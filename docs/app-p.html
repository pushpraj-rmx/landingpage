IMT Ghaziabad Digital Marketing Details

Explore different concepts of Digital Marketing like Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Social Media, Pay-Per-Click (PPC), Website Conversion Optimization, Web Analytics, Marketing Analytics, Content, Email and Mobile Marketing in this course.

Learning Path

  • Orientation Session for IMT Ghaziabad Digital Marketing

    Get started with the Digital Marketing Professional Certificate program with IMT Ghaziabad and Simplilearn. Kickstart your professional digital marketing journey with a foundational course.

  • PC DM - Introduction to Digital Marketing

    Begin your journey towards reinventing digital marketing strategies for a business to get better engagement and attract customers through this course. Learn the skills, insights, and digital know-how to become a high-performance digital marketer.

  • PC DM - Search Engine Optimization: Optimize your online visibility

    Increase your online visibility with our advanced SEO course. Explore the different facets of SEO and learn topics like SEO optimization, keyword management, and research, on-page and off-page optimization, link building, URL building, and SEO analytics to increase engagement for your business.

    Preview
  • PC DM - Pay Per Click: An online internet model

    This Pay-Per-Click course will show you how you can develop and execute an advanced PPC strategy that will drive results for the businesses you are representing. Also get hands-on experience in managing paid marketing initiatives.

    Preview
  • PC DM - Social Media Marketing: Connect with your audience

    Become a social media marketing expert with help of this course. Master the various aspects of social media marketing including strategy, reputation management, influencer marketing, content marketing, and web analytics.

    Preview
  • PC DM - Web Analytics: Understanding user behavior

    Study online user behavior and activities to generate valuable marketing intelligence with this web analytics course. This course will help you improve your organization’s ability to make highly-informed business decisions based on data analytics.

    Preview
  • PC DM - Marketing Analytics: Optimize your campaigns

    This course introduces you to the essentials of analyzing unstructured social media data and its applications in marketing and managerial decision-making. You will learn to make use of social media text and network data to understand consumer behaviour, sentiment, and attitude. It will also provide an initial foundation for pursuing your career in the analytics and data science domains, in addition to its application in the digital marketing domain.

    Preview
  • PC DM - Digital Marketing Capstone Project

    The Digital Marketing Capstone project will give you an opportunity to implement the concepts and skills you learned in the Professional Certificate in Digital Marketing. Through dedicated mentoring sessions from IMT Ghaziabad faculty, you’ll learn how to solve real-world, industry-aligned case studies.

Electives:
  • Brand Management

    Understand the principles and concepts of brand management. It will help you build brands for companies, implement marketing strategies, plan and execute branding campaigns, and successfully manage them.

  • Advanced Mobile Marketing

    Become an expert in using mobile as a means of marketing communications. Master the art of mobile advertising, responsive designs, and integrated social media marketing to become an industry-ready mobile marketer through this mobile marketing course.

  • Advanced Email Marketing

    This Advanced Email Marketing Certification Training course helps you master advanced email marketing techniques and strategies. Learn how to draft an email and develop expertise in creating effective advertisements and brand awareness, and building customer trust and loyalty.

  • Digital Marketing Meta Master Session

    Attend online interactive masterclasses from Meta and get insights about advancements in the digital marketing field.

  • Advanced Content Marketing

    Become an expert in content marketing. Master various aspects of planning and executing content marketing strategies to create impressive content with greater efficiency and impact with this Advanced Content Marketing Certification course.

  • Advanced Website Conversion Rate Optimization

    Become an expert in conversion optimization through this Advanced Website Conversion Rate Optimization Certification Training course. Learn how you can use an effective framework to help convert your website’s visitors into sales leads and gain a firm understanding of online marketing.

IMT Ghaziabad Digital Marketing FAQs

  • What are the eligibility criteria for this Professional Digital Marketing Online Program by IMT Ghaziabad?

    For admission to this Professional Digital Marketing Program, candidates must possess the following criteria:

    • At least 2 years of work experience
    • A bachelor's degree in any discipline
    • Digital marketing background is desirable but not required

  • What is the admission process for this Professional Digital Marketing Online Program in partnership with the IMT Ghaziabad?

    The admission process for this Professional Digital Marketing program consists of three easy steps:

    • Interested candidates will need to apply by submitting the application form online
    • Candidates will be shortlisted by an admission panel based on the application submitted
    • Selected candidates will be awarded an offer of admission that they can accept by paying the program fee

  • What are the benefits of IMT Ghaziabad Associate Alumni Membership?

    Below are the benefits of IMT Ghaziabad Associate Alumni Membership:

    • 20% discount on MDPs offered by IMT Ghaziabad (short or long term)
    • Email ID issued by the IT department of IMT Ghaziabad
    • In person library access for 15 days within a year of passing the program (no remote access)
    • Information and communication on the events and seminars conducted by IMT Ghaziabad

  • Will financial aid be provided for this Professional Certificate in Digital Marketing?

    To ensure money is not a limiting factor in learning, we offer various financing options to help make this professional certificate in digital marketing program financially manageable. For more details, please refer to our "Admissions Fee and Financing" section.

  • What should I expect from the Professional Certificate in Digital Marketing?

    As a part of this Professional Digital Marketing Certificate Online Program, you will receive the following:

    • Professional Certificate in Digital Marketing by IMT Ghaziabad
    • IMT Ghaziabad Associate Alumni status
    • Live classes delivered by IMT faculty
    • Lifetime access to all core eLearning content created by Simplilearn

  • What certificate will I receive?

    Upon completion of this Digital Marketing program, you will be awarded a Professional Certificate in Digital Marketing by IMT Ghaziabad. You will also receive industry-recognized certifications from Simplilearn for the courses included in the learning path.

  • What is the Hootsuite Platform Training course and how will I earn its Certificate?

    We partnered with the Hootsuite Academy team to include the Hootsuite Platform Training course as part of the Digital Marketing program. In this module, you’ll develop the skills to use core Hootsuite products to their full potential, boosting the impact of your social media efforts, driving results, and proving your ROI.

    Once you've completed the Hootsuite Platform Training, you'll be ready to take the exam on the Hootsuite Academy website to become a Hootsuite Certified Professional. As a Simplilearn student, you'll get 50% off on this industry-recognized credential! Get certified for 50% off.

  • What is SE Ranking platform access and how can I avail it?

    As a part of this digital marketing bootcamp, you will get free access to the SE Ranking platform for the validity of 1-year. Once you complete the Search Engine Optimization module (the second module) of the Digital Marketing Training program, you will get an exclusive promo code from Simplilearn to access the platform.

  • Will any preparation material be provided as part of this professional Digital Marketing program to help me get started?

    As soon as you make the payment for the program fee’s first installment, you will gain access to a preparatory course. You are expected to go through the assigned course before attending your first digital marketing course class. The Professional Digital Marketing course will have eight to ten hours of self-paced learning content in the form of videos.

  • What is Global Teaching Assistance?

    Our teaching assistants are a dedicated team of subject matter experts to help you get certified on your first attempt. They proactively engage with you to ensure adherence to the course path and enrich your learning experience, from class onboarding to project mentoring and job assistance.

  • Who are the instructors for this Digital Marketing Certification training and how are they selected?

    Our highly-qualified instructors are industry experts with years of relevant industry experience. Each of them undergoes a rigorous selection process, including profile screening, technical evaluation, and a training demo evaluation before becoming certified to train for us. Besides, we ensure that only those trainers with a high alumni rating remain part of our faculty.

  • What is the JobAssist program?

    Simplilearn’s JobAssist program is an India-specific offering in collaboration with IIMJobs.com to help you land your dream job. With the JobAssist program, we offer extended support for certified learners looking for a job change or starting their first job. Upon completing this professional digital marketing certificate program, you will be eligible to apply for JobAssist, and your details will be shared with IIMJobs. As a part of this program, IIMJobs will offer the following exclusive services:

    • IIMJobs pro-membership for six months
    • Resume-building assistance
    • Career mentoring
    • Interview preparation
    • Career fairs

  • I am not able to access the online digital marketing certificate program. Who can help me?

    Contact us using the form on the right side of any page on the Simplilearn website, select the Live Chat link, or contact Help & Support.

  • What is covered under the 24/7 Support guarantee?

    We offer 24/7 support through email, chat, and calls through our dedicated team, and provide on-demand assistance through our community forum. What’s more, you will have lifetime access to the community forum, even after completing your Digital Marketing program.

  • If I want to cancel my enrollment in this Professional Digital Marketing course, can I get a refund?

    Yes, you can cancel your enrollment if necessary. We will refund the program price after deducting an administration fee. To learn more, please read our Refund Policy.


    * Disclaimer
    All programs are offered on a non-credit basis and are not transferable to a degree.

  • Can I get a sealed transcript for World Education Services (WES) at the end of the program?

    Professional Certificate Program are certification programs and do not include any transcripts for WES, this is reserved only for degree programs. We do not offer sealed transcripts and hence, our programs are not applicable for WES or similar services.
